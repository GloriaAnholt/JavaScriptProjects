Edible Mushrooms of the Pacific Northwest

While no one knows for certain how many varieties of mushrooms exist in nature, there are about 10,000 species in North America which have been described. Luckily the Pacific Northwest is home to over a dozen delicious edible species, many of which can be easily and safely identified.

Let's see if your finds are one of these varieties! Select the outline of your mushroom to get started.
